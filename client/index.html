PrepMe can help you prepare for your upcoming interview by generating potential interview questions based on the job posting you paste in the textbox. You can then record your audio response to each question and submit it for evaluation. PrepMe will provide feedback on your performance, including areas where you can improve using STAR format.

  • The STAR (Situation, Task, Action, and Result) format is a commonly used technique for answering behavioral interview questions. It can help you provide clear and structured answers for interviews.
