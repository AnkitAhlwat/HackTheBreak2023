Nail Your Next Interview!

PrepBot can help you prepare for your upcoming interview by generating potential interview questions based on the job posting you paste in the textbox. You can then record your audio response to each question and submit it for evaluation. PrepBot will provide feedback on your performance, including areas where you can improve using STAR (Situation, Task, Action, and Result) format.
